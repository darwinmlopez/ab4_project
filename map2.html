  • The spread of electric vehicles in Washington State is well-represented visually by the dot density map. The map helps to identify regions with strong rates of electric vehicle adoption and shows possible hotspots for future charging infrastructure development by showing clusters of 50 electric vehicles. Policymakers, companies, and individuals that want to encourage the use of electric vehicles and assist the creation of sustainable transportation systems must know this information.
  • The dot density map can also be used to indicate potential obstacles to the use of electric vehicles, such as a lack of infrastructure for charging or restricted access to incentives and rebates. With the help of this information, customized policies and outreach initiatives may be created to remove these obstacles and promote a wider uptake of electric vehicles in particular regions. The map can also be used to guide decisions regarding the placement of new charging stations and other relevant infrastructure, ensuring that they are put where they are most required and will have the biggest impact.
